BioGraphs builds heavily on [Jinsect][jinsect] and [JGraphT][jgrapht]. It released under the GPLv3 license.

#### Requirements
* Apache Commons v4.0
* JGraphT v0.9.1
* BioJava v4.0.0
* JInsect v1.1-rc (a release can be found [here][release])
